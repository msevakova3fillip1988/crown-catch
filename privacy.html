The App is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

3. Information we collect

We collect only the limited information necessary to operate the App and measure our marketing:

  • Network information. IP address.
  • Push notification token. A token issued by your device, used only to deliver notifications if you have enabled them.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or any other content from your device, except as expressly stated above.

  • To comply with legal obligations.
